Works in TTY, suport files and remote videos

mpv /path/to/file.jpeg -> Opens image
mpv /path/to/file.mpv -> Opens video
mpv https://youtu.be/JHsdf74s -> Open remote youtube video

o -> Show interface
9-0 -> Increase or decrease volume
Arrow or Shift+Arrow -> Jump 5, 10 or 60 seconds
m -> Mute 
s -> Screenshot
j -> Subtitles
q -> Quit

To get help, press "?" (Shift + /)
